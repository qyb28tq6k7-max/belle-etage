The Story

Built by someone
living this chapter.

I earned my Bachelor of Science in Nutrition and Chemistry from the University of Arizona — years spent learning how the body works at a molecular level, and how the food and compounds we put into it shape everything that follows. Then perimenopause arrived, and I realized how little good information existed for women like me. Not clinical papers. Not fear-based blog posts. Real, nuanced, luxurious guidance for a woman who has spent her whole life taking care of herself and everyone else.

Belle Étage — French for "the beautiful floor" — is the name given to the most coveted level of a Parisian Haussmannian building: higher ceilings, taller windows, the best light. It is the floor reserved for living beautifully. That is what this chapter of life can be, too. Every supplement recommendation comes with the mechanism. Every beauty tool comes with the research. Every hat recommendation comes with the UPF rating. Every conversation about intimacy comes without shame. Because you deserve to know why.

— Belle Étage

Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: May 2026

Belle Étage participates in affiliate programs with brands and retailers whose products meet our standards for quality, safety, and science. When you click certain links on this site or in our newsletter and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at no additional cost to you.

How we choose what to recommend

Affiliate relationships never determine what we cover or recommend. Every product featured is independently selected based on the research, the formulation, the manufacturing standards, and — wherever possible — direct personal use. If a brand pays well but the product is not worth your money, you will not find it here.

Who we partner with

Our current affiliate partners include practitioner-grade supplement brands (such as Thorne and Designs for Health), clinically-cleared beauty devices (such as NuFACE and Bon Charge), premium activewear and movement platforms, and curated sun protection brands. The list evolves as our coverage grows.

Sponsored content

From time to time, Belle Étage may publish a sponsored newsletter issue or post. These are always clearly labeled at the top of the piece. Sponsored does not mean unvetted: a brand cannot pay for a sponsorship unless their product would have earned a recommendation on its own merit.

A note on medical claims

Belle Étage is a wellness publication, not a medical practice. Content is for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before starting any new supplement, device, or protocol — especially during perimenopause and menopause.

Your Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Belle Étage ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our site or subscribe to our newsletter, how we use it, and the choices you have. We collect as little as possible, and we never sell your personal information.

What we collect

Information you give us. When you subscribe to the newsletter, you provide your first name and email address. If you write to us, we receive whatever you choose to share in your message.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we receive basic technical information from your browser — IP address, device type, pages visited, referring site, and timestamps — primarily to understand site performance and improve content.

Cookies. We use a small number of cookies for analytics and to remember basic preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; the site will still work.

How we use it

We use your information to deliver the newsletter you subscribed to, respond to your messages, understand which content resonates, and improve the site. That is the entire list. We do not use your email to target advertising.

Who we share it with

We share information only with the service providers that help us operate — for example, our email platform (to deliver newsletters), our analytics tool (to count visits), and our hosting provider. Each is bound by their own privacy commitments, and none receives information they do not need to do their job.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Ever.

Affiliate links

Some product links on this site and in our newsletters are affiliate links. When you click one, the destination retailer may set their own cookies and may share back limited information about whether a purchase occurred. See our Affiliate Disclosure for details.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the link in the footer of every email. You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to delete it, by emailing Jaimeeschultz@gmail.com. We honor these requests promptly.

Children

Belle Étage is intended for adult readers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is perfectly secure, but we work with reputable providers and limit access to the minimum number of people necessary.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify subscribers by email.

Contact

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Write to Jaimeeschultz@gmail.com.

## HOW MONEY IS MADE

### 1. Affiliate Commissions (Primary — start here)
Recommend products in newsletters and on the website. When readers buy, you earn 10-25% commission. No inventory. No shipping. Pure passive income.

**Estimated earnings per 1,000 subscribers:**
- 1 newsletter/week × 44% open rate × 8% click rate = ~35 clicks
- 35 clicks × 12% average conversion = ~4 sales
- 4 sales × $80 average order × 18% commission = ~$58/newsletter
- At 4 newsletters/month: ~$230/month per 1,000 subscribers

**At 5,000 subscribers: ~$1,150/month**
**At 10,000 subscribers: ~$2,300/month**
**At 25,000 subscribers: ~$5,750/month**

### 2. Digital Products (Month 3+)
- "The Perimenopause Protocol" guide: $47
- "Red Light Therapy for Beginners" video course: $97

### 3. Sponsored Newsletter Issues (Month 6+)
Once you hit 5,000+ subscribers, brands will pay $500-2,000 per sponsored issue.

1. **Nutrition and chemistry credibility** — most wellness influencers have no scientific background. You have a BS in Nutrition and Chemistry from the University of Arizona. Lead with it every time.
2. **Honest affiliate approach** — you evaluate products the way a nutrition scientist would: ingredient by ingredient, mechanism by mechanism. Your readers trust you because you tell them the limitations, not just the benefits.
3. **Perimenopause specificity** — the 40-65 demographic is wealthy, underserved, and desperate for a trusted voice. You are that voice.
